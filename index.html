How much does a website cost?

We understand that every business has different needs, budgets, and flexibility. Enysite is committed to working with you to find the best outcome with the constraints.

We offer two pricing models: fixed fee and retainer. Following discovery conversations and desired outcomes, we work together to determine the model most suitable for your business needs.

Cost of a website varies greatly based on the complexity. However, all basic websites charged on a fixed cost starts at $5,000. While a website rich with features and many pages will could require up to $15,000 or more.

Retained services start at a minimum of $500 per month; but could require $2000 or more monthly based on the project's complexity.

How long does it take to make a website?

On average, a web design and development project can take between 4 to 8 weeks to complete. However, a project with more complexity might require 12 weeks or more. Keep in mind that there could be some flexibility in the timeline depending on your specific goals and needs. If you have a project that needs to be completed within a shorter timeframe, it's always worth reaching out to discuss the possibilities.
